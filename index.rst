About
=====

NeuralNetwork_lib is a Java Library made to easily construct
Neural Networks and later on even Convolutional Neural networks and LSTM's.

It is a project by Erik Hammon who tries to gain more knowledge about the inner
workings of Neural networks by building this Library.
